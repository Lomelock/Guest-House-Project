About Us

Welcome to Naibala Lake Natron Lodge, where comfort meets adventure in one of Tanzania's most extraordinary destinations! Nestled in the heart of the Lake Natron region, we serve as the perfect base for exploring the wonders of this unique landscape. Our lodge specializes in hosting guests who are passionate about nature exploration, cultural immersion, and creating unforgettable memories.

Founded with a vision to provide authentic, sustainable tourism experiences, Naibala Lake Natron Lodge combines comfortable accommodation with access to world-class attractions. From the otherworldly pink flamingos to the sacred Ol Doinyo Lengai volcano, from pristine waterfall hikes to genuine Maasai cultural encounters, we help you experience the full spectrum of Lake Natron's magic.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes! Groups of 6 or more people receive a 10% discount on accommodation rates. Groups of 15+ receive 15% discount. Contact us directly via WhatsApp for group booking arrangements and custom packages.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 7 days before arrival receive a full refund. Cancellations within 7 days are subject to a 50% cancellation fee. No-shows are charged in full. We recommend travel insurance for your bookings.

Are there peak season prices?

Yes, pricing may increase by 15-20% during peak season (June-September and December-January). Low season discounts of up to 10% are available April-May and October-November. Contact us for current rates.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, mobile money (M-Pesa, Airtel Money), and cash payments. A 30% deposit is required to confirm bookings, with the balance due 7 days before arrival.

Do you cater to special dietary requirements?

Absolutely! We accommodate vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and other dietary requirements. Please inform us at least 48 hours in advance so our chef can prepare appropriate meals.

Can I extend my stay?

Yes, we can often accommodate extended stays subject to availability. Weekly stays receive a 5% discount, and monthly stays receive 10% discount. Contact us to arrange longer accommodations.

What is included in tour prices?

Our tour prices include professional guided tours, transportation where applicable, and park entrance fees. Meals and drinks during tours are typically not included unless specified. Check tour details or ask our staff.

Do you have family or children packages?

Yes! Children under 5 stay free with full board guests, and receive 10% discount on B&B and room only rates. Cots and high chairs are available upon request. Many of our activities are suitable for families.
